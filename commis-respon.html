Responsibilities

  1. Cleans, cuts and prepares all fruits, vegetables, herbs, and salad items

  2. Cooks foods such as bacon, potatoes, eggs, vegetables, fruits, and pasta

  3. Prepares dishes such as sandwiches, salads & vegetables

  4. Serves food according to correct taste, temperature and visual appeal

  5. Maintains the area in a clean, safe and sanitary manner

  6. Maintains food safety temperature logs with direction from Lead Cook and Sous Chef

  7. Reads and refers to cooking reference material

  8. Completes assigned tasks as quickly as is safely practical

  9. Follows the resorts policies and procedures and adheres to the grooming policies

  10. Other duties as required
