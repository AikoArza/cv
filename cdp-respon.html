Responsibilities

  1. Take care of daily food preparation and duties assigned by the superiors to meet the standard and the quality set by the Restaurant.

  2. Follow the instructions and recommendations from the immediate superiors to complete the daily tasks.

  3. Coordinate daily tasks with the Sous Chef.

  4. To supervise junior chefs or commis.

  5. To estimate the daily production needs and checking the quality of raw and cooked food products to ensure that standards are met.

  6. Ensure that the production, preparation and presentation of food are of the highest quality at all times.

  7. Ensure highest levels of guest satisfaction, quality, operating and food costs on an ongoing basis.

  8. To be full aware of all menu items, their recipes, methods of production and presentation standards.

  9. Follow good preservation standards for the proper handling of all food products at the right temperature.

  10. Operate and maintain all department equipment and reporting of malfunctioning.

  11. To be responsible for hygiene, safety and correct use of equipment and utensils.
